# CRSCPH Website

Welcome to the official repository for the **Center for Research on Social Norms and Systems Change in Public Health (CRSCPH)**.  
This site hosts the public-facing website for CRSCPH’s research, consulting, and public health leadership initiatives.

## About CRSCPH
CRSCPH is dedicated to advancing public health through rigorous social norms research, systems transformation, and leadership development.  
Our work integrates evidence-driven inquiry with community-grounded approaches to support meaningful, sustainable change in public health systems.

## What This Website Includes
- Overview of CRSCPH’s mission, values, and focus areas  
- Social norms research and publications  
- Systems change frameworks and thought leadership  
- Public health consulting services  
- Leadership development offerings  
- Contact and engagement information  

## Built With
- **Quarto** – for reproducible, research-friendly website development  
- **RStudio / VS Code** – for local development  
- **Cloudflare Pages** – for secure, fast hosting  
- **GitHub** – version control and collaboration  

## Deployment
This site is deployed automatically through **Cloudflare Pages**.  
Whenever updates are pushed to the `main` branch, Cloudflare rebuilds and deploys the site.

## How to Contribute
This is a managed professional website.  
If you wish to recommend updates or edits, please submit a request or open an issue.
